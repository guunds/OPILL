About This Vault

The svtBTC vault coordinates capital for intent-based tBTC settlement. LPs deposit tBTC on OP_NET, and that liquidity powers solvers. Fees generated from solver activity flow back to depositors monthly.

Strategy Details
  • Deposit tBTC on OP_NET → receive svtBTC vault receipt token
  • Stake svtBTC to earn $OPILL rewards (30d min lock)
  • Capital bridges to opill-treasury via cross-chain governance
  • Monthly distribution of solver fees + principal back to vault
